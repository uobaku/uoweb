YeloBank English Workshop

Our first event took place at the Yelobank Head Office on August 4, 2023. The event featured a combination of social and educational activities designed to engage children with limited abilities. Moreover, the event included English workshops aimed at enhancing language skills and fostering a welcoming learning environment.

ISOPS Summer Camp

Our ISOPS Camp Event was organized in collaboration with the Bilgah Beach Hotel and ISOPS Summer Schools, held in the summer camp on August 22, 2023. The event incorporated various social activities focusing on architecture and creative art. We offered master classes and mentoring sessions for the children in attendance, in order to enrich their camp experience.

EcoTech Exhibition

Our third event, Ecotech, held on September 9, 2023, at the prestigious Landmark Rotunda Hall, was a result of a collaborative effort between Uşaqlarımıza Öyrədək and Ecotech teams. The event showcased a thematic exhibition centered on ecology and technology, featuring projects of participants spanning various age groups.

ETSN Event

Held on October 7th in Shirvan Milli Park with the support of the Ministry of Ecology and Natural Resources of Azerbaijan, Uşaqlarımıza Öyrədək held an event dedicated to the importance of ecology, which we have achieved through eco masterclasses with underprivileged children.

The Autizmabid

The Autizmabid Event aims to nurture the creative potential of children with limited abilities of all age groups and all regions of our precious Republic of Azerbaijan. Our aspiration is to pave the way for a brighter future for these exceptional children, giving them opportunities they did not have.

Rebuild Karabakh Event

Our 6th event, known as the Rebuild Karabakh Expo, was made possible through the support of Umid Var and the Caspian Event Organizers Group (CEO Group). The event encouraged support for underprivileged and disabled children from multiple regions of our beloved Republic of Azerbaijan.

Azercell Massali Event

The Azercell Masalli Event was a significant initiative made possible through collaboration with Azercell Telecom LLC in the Masalli region of the Republic of Azerbaijan. This event held special significance as it paid homage to martyrs and their families whilst delving into the rich history of the Karabakh region.

Medinex Exhibition

One of our recent events, "Medinex" exhibition took place at the Baku Expo Center from November 2 to 4, 2023. The event’s primary objective was to provide children from several regions of the Republic of Azerbaijan with limited abilities of all ages with a platform that showcased and recognized their art and exhibits.

Baku International Book Fair Expo

Our organization proudly participated in the Baku Book Fair, on November 11-15, with the invaluable support of Umid Var. At this event, we placed a special emphasis on children with limited abilities. The event was organized with the support of the Ministry of Culture of the Republic of Azerbaijan.

Landau Fundraiser

The Landau Fundraiser event was hosted on the prestigious premises of Landau Real Estate and brings us closer to supporting local underprivileged children from across Azerbaijan in a charity-filled atmosphere. It was one of our most crucial events, with the goal of providing tangible support to children in need.

UO Teambuilding

Team Building of Uşaqlarımıza Öyrədək Team in Fusion Hub Restaurant, on December 29th dedicated to the New Year Celebration and Team qualification.

World Scholar's Cup

We proudly represented our Organization at the World Scholar’s Cup and got support from both the European Azerbaijan School and WSC Organizational Team for future events, we successfully organized a media campaign and gave out our merchandise to participants of WSC.

Baku Decides

Our President and Head Organizer proudly represented Uşaqlarımıza Öyrədək at “Baku Decides” 1st Youth Climate Negotiations, where they observed talks of international delegations and the UN. We got supported by more than 5 country delegations and 17 organizations, including the Minister of Youth and Sports and the Minister of Environment and Natural Resources of the Republic of Azerbaijan. The conference lasted 4 days from January 29th to February 1st, and it was the first step to COP29.

Hack4Good Hackathon

H4G Charity Hackathon was organized by the team of Hack4Good on February 3rd, 2024. Our volunteers helped with the event, where students from various schools represented their IT and AI-based projects at Landau School and were qualified for several nominations.
